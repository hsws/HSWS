About Us

Help Social welfare Society – is a Registered Organisation under Society Registration act 1860 in 2011 . It is a Uttar Pradesh based non-profit non- governmental organization. While quoting Martin Luther King Jr. Our president /Founder says,“An individual has not started living until he or she can rise above the narrow confines of individualistic concerns to the broader concerns of all humanity.’’ In a short period, he has outstretched towards like-minded youth who strongly believe in working selflessly for improving our Nation.
